Narendra Modi – Prime Minister of India
Full Name: Narendra Damodardas Modi
Date of Birth: 17 September 1950
Political Party: Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)
Current Position: 14th and current Prime Minister of India
Tenure: Since 26 May 2014
Constituency: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

🛤️ Early Life & Background
Born in Vadnagar, Gujarat, into a modest family.

Helped his father sell tea at a railway station in his childhood (often cited in his life story).

Joined Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) in his youth — a right-wing, Hindu nationalist organization that later influenced his political ideology.
